About Yaritza

Hi! I have been living in New York City since I was a kid, and I love it. There is so much to do and experience, that I am convinced getting bored in this city is a matter of personal choice.

I like hiking, biking, exploring ethnic cuisines, plays, checking out chamber music, and taking scenic pictures. When the weather permits, my heart is happy with just plopping down on a lush patch of grass to people watch, or picnic, and making art with friends. Come snow days, and I like to be in Central Park crunching on snow and taking in the winter wonderland.

On the professional side of things, I have a combined 7 years of experience working as a clinical laboratory technologist doing both genetic and Cancer screenings. For a long time however, I had been pining for a career change but I was uncertain of which path to follow. This summer it dawned on me to go into programming. I am training to be a Ruby full-stack web developer.

So why programming? It appeals to me on so many levels. It can satisfy my desire for stimulating creative work, my want for a career that would allow me to work remotely, as well as the opportunity for better financial reward to share with family. It is relevant to almost any industry that I can think of. Basically, I had seen how positively life-changing having programming as a tool could be in the life of people around me, and I hoped it will do the same for me.

Currently I am a student just starting to dip my fingers into programming at General Assembly. I am excited about the journey and learning myself into a new chapter in life. I am also feeling super grateful to be part of the Ruby community which is proving to be supportive, and collaborative. To family and friends who have helped make my transition into student-hood easier —Thank you!!!
